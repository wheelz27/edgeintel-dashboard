The Model

How EdgeIntel Works

EdgeIntel is a sports analytics engine that identifies betting edges — situations where the model's projected outcome differs meaningfully from what the market is pricing. Here's exactly how it works.

Step 1 — Time-Weighted Power Ratings

Every team gets a power rating calculated from four time windows:

SEASON RATING
15% weight
Full season baseline
L15 GAMES
25% weight
Recent momentum
L5 GAMES
35% weight
Hot/cold streaks
LAST GAME
25% weight
Latest form signal
Step 2 — Projection Engine

The composite power ratings feed a projection model that outputs a predicted score for each team. These projections are adjusted for home court advantage, pace, injury impact, and rest differentials. The projected spread and total are then compared against current market lines.

Step 3 — Edge Detection + Pick Selection

When the model's projected spread diverges from the market spread by a threshold amount, that gap is the "edge." The edge percentage is calculated as the gap divided by the typical variance for that sport.

Only games where the edge exceeds the threshold AND confidence is high enough get designated as Edge Picks. Every day, exactly 2-3 games make this cut — these are the only plays that count toward the official record.

Scotty AI — Your Analyst

Scotty is an AI analyst powered by Claude that gets fed the real game data: both teams' power ratings, the model projection, the live odds, injury reports, and the edge calculation. Ask Scotty anything about a game — "What kills this bet?", "Is this still a play at -4.5?", "Sizing?" — and get a direct, concise answer ending with ACTION: bet/pass/wait/hedge.
